About Naoshi Mizuta

Who is he?

Naoshi Mizuta was born in 1973 in Kochi, Japen. He attended Chiba University where he studied law and economics. After graduation, Naoshi joined Capcom to compose music for ceratin games

Naoshi saw a Sqaure Enix recruitment ad which prompted him to leave Capcom and work for Sqaure Enix. While at Sqaure, he composed music for the Final Fantasy Series as their main composer.

He also has his own band called The Star Onions.

Discography

Musical Works

Naoshi is best known for the following compilations:

  • Final Fantasy XI, 2002
  • Final Fantsy XI expansions to include:
    1. Rise of Zilart, 2003
    2. Chains of Promathia, 2004
    3. Treasures of Aht Urghan, 2006
    4. Wings of the Goddess, 2007
    5. Seekers of Adoulin, 2013
  • Final Fantasy XIII-2, 2011
  • Final Fantasy XIV: A Real Reborn, 2013
  • Lightning Returns: Final Fantasy XIII, 2013
